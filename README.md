# Customer_Behavior-Analysis
# 🛍️ Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing customer shopping behavior to uncover patterns, trends, and key factors that influence purchasing decisions. The goal is to help businesses improve customer engagement, increase sales, and build long-term customer loyalty.

---

## ❓ Business Problem

A retail company wants to better understand its customers’ shopping behavior due to noticeable changes in purchasing patterns across:

* Demographics
* Product categories
* Sales channels (Online vs Offline)

### 🎯 Key Question:

**How can the company leverage consumer shopping data to identify trends, improve customer engagement, and optimize marketing and product strategies?**

---

## ⚙️ Project Workflow

### 1. 🧹 Data Preparation & Modeling (Python)

* Cleaned missing and inconsistent data
* Transformed raw dataset into analysis-ready format
* Handled duplicates and data quality issues

### 2. 🗄️ Data Analysis (SQL)

* Structured data into tables
* Simulated business transactions
* Performed analysis on:

  * Customer segmentation (New, Returning, Loyal)
  * Purchase frequency
  * Revenue contribution
  * Buying patterns

### 3. 📊 Visualization (Power BI)

* Built an interactive dashboard
* Visualized key metrics and trends

### 4. 📑 Report & Presentation

* Summarized insights and findings
* Provided actionable business recommendations

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

* Customer Segmentation Analysis
* Sales Trends (Time & Season)
* Top Products & Revenue Insights
* Customer Purchase Behavior
* Impact of Discounts, Reviews & Payment Methods

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Loyal customers contribute the highest revenue
* Discounts and seasonal trends significantly impact sales
* Certain product categories dominate customer preferences
* Reviews and payment methods influence purchase decisions

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Focus on retaining loyal customers with reward programs
* Use targeted discounts during peak seasons
* Optimize product offerings based on demand trends
* Improve customer experience using feedback and reviews

---

* **Python** (Data Cleaning & Preparation)
* **SQL** (Data Analysis)
* **Power BI** (Data Visualization)
